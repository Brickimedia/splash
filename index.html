What is Brickimedia?

Brickimedia is an open network of LEGO fan communities founded in early 2013. In addition to a coordination wiki, Brickimedia hosts 4 projects: Brickipedia, Customs, LEGO Stories Wiki, and LEGO CUUSOO Wiki.

Brickimedia was originally launched in February 2013, but several months later the site's data was lost during an attack on its webhost. Since that downtime, Brickimedia has been through a closed alpha and closed beta, and is currently in an open beta testing period.
